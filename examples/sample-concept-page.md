# Compiled knowledge

## Definition
Knowledge that has been selectively transformed from raw material into canonical, reusable pages.

## Why it matters
It reduces repeated reading and improves future answers.

## Key distinctions
- not raw notes
- not full automation
- not vector retrieval alone
